My favorite song of the moment


Smart by Le Sserafim

"Smart" is a side track from a new album that this korean group has just dropped out on the market.
This song is 2:46 minutes long, and it has a very fresh and summery vibe to it. This has made help make it really popular among listeners from all around the world aside from it's choreography.

Le Sserafim

Le Sserafim is a South Korean girl group formed by Source Music.
It is made up of five members: Sakura, Chaewon, Yunjin, Kazuha and Eunchae. The group made their official debut on May 2, 2022 with the EP Fearless.

Album "Easy"

Easy is their third EP. It was released by Source Music on February 19, 2024 and contains five tracks, including the lead single of the same name.
